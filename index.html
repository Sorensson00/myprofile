France > Sweden ?

Even though I introduced myself as enjoying Paris, the culture and the food, I thank Sweden for IKEA! Not only have I been able to buy some things for my apartment, but IKEA saves you when you are craving for some swedish meatballs with mashed potatoes, lingonberry and gravy.

France is famous for its cheeses, however it's not quite the cheeses we have on our sandwiches in sweden - IKEA comes to the rescue ones again. What you really need here in France is an osthyvel, I can't believe how you survive without one!
